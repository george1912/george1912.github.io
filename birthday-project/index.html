The Birthday Project

A little bit of backstory for this project: originally, the author was not able to make it to a birthday party, and that absence stayed with him. He wanted to make something special, the kind of thing that felt equal to the value of the friendship. Of course, like most big ideas from the heart, the first version grew into something huge. It was going to be a website, a whole experience, a gift with a lot of moving parts.

This time, the scope became quieter and more personal. The goal was simple: make something by hand, make it feel honest, and let it say what can be hard to say out loud. This project is a small virtual canvas, made to be read slowly, listened to, and kept.

About the author

A Journey Is Coming to a Close

A note from the author: part of making this piece has been reconnecting with his roots and remembering the kind of person he wants to be. He is currently moving through his last semester of nursing school, finally nearing the end of a long road while facing big decisions and big changes. He apologizes for his absence, but hopes it is always known that he is there whenever he is needed. For now, he is trying to make it to the beach this summer and breathe a little before the next chapter begins.
